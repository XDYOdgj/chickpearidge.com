Cozeyat Flameless Mother's Day Candles

These Cozeyat Mother's Day candles are a beautiful and safe way to add warmth and ambiance to any room. Designed with a premium frosted plastic shell, they emit a soft, warm glow that creates a calming and romantic atmosphere. Whether you're looking to decorate your home, host a party, or gift someone special, these candles offer the perfect blend of style and functionality.

Each candle comes in a unique size, making them a versatile choice for various decor styles. With a remote control feature, you can conveniently change colors and set timers for 4 or 8 hours, allowing you to customize the lighting experience however you like. Their battery-operated design means no need for candles, matches, or open flames, ensuring safe and easy operation in any setting.
