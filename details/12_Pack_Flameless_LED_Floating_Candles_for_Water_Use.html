12 Flameless LED Floating Candles

Elevate your special occasions with these elegant and safe flameless floating candles. Designed for use in water, they bring a touch of magic and ambiance to weddings, pool parties, and holiday decor without the hazards of open flame.

Key Features

  • Realistic flickering effect that mimics real candles
  • Waterproof plastic construction for durability and safety
  • Long-lasting battery operation lasting over 100 hours
  • Automatic on/off feature activates with water contact
  • Stable design ensures upright placement on moving water

Why Choose These Candles?

Whether you're planning an intimate wedding or a lively outdoor gathering, these candles are perfect for creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. They're easy to set up, require no lighting sources, and are safe for use around family and guests. Versatile & Ready to Use

Each candle comes with a built-in battery, so you can enjoy them straight out of the box. Their versatility allows them to be used both indoors and outdoors, making them an essential addition to any themed event or celebration.
