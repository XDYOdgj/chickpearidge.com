Hanzim Flameless Taper Candles

Add a touch of elegance and safety to your space with these beautifully crafted flameless candles.

The durable plastic construction ensures a dripless and safe experience for both homes and events. With a remote control, you can adjust brightness and set timers for 2, 4, 6, or 8 hours, making them the perfect choice for displays in hard-to-reach areas or long-lasting decor.

These flameless candles are ideal for weddings, parties, holidays, and more. They're not only stylish but also worry-free, especially in spaces where kids or pets are present. Use them in your fireplace, window, table center, or even bathroom for a magical and safe ambiance.
