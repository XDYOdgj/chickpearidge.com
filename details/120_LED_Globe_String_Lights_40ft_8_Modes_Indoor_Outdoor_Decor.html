Create a warm, inviting ambiance with these 120 LED globe string lights

Featuring frosted globes, these lights emit a soft and cozy glow that's gentle on the eyes, perfect for adding a magical touch to any space. Each globe offers a unique lighting effect that can't be replicated by other string lights, ensuring a special and eye-catching display.

Versatile and functional design

Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, these lights are ideal for holiday, wedding, and party decorations. They effortlessly transform your home or outdoor space into a festive haven, whether you're hosting an event or simply creating a cozy atmosphere.

Smart lighting features

With an easy-to-use button on the plug, you can seamlessly switch between 8 different lighting modes, including combination, waves, sequential, and chasing/flash. Perfect for every occasion

From Christmas decoration lights to festive party lighting, these 120 LED globe string lights add a touch of warmth and charm to any setting. Their 40ft length with an extra 10ft lead wire provides flexibility for easy setup, making them an essential item for homes and events alike.
